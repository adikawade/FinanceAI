# FinanceAI — Financial Health & Net Worth Engine

**Document Version:** 1.0.0

## Overview
The Financial Health Engine continuously evaluates a user's financial position using multiple weighted indicators.

## Net Worth Formula
Net Worth = Total Assets − Total Liabilities

## Health Score Factors
- Savings Rate
- Emergency Fund
- Debt Ratio
- Investment Activity
- Budget Discipline
- Income Stability
- Goal Progress
- Net Worth Trend
- Tax Readiness
- Spending Behaviour

## Health Levels
- 0–20 Critical
- 21–40 Poor
- 41–60 Average
- 61–80 Good
- 81–100 Excellent
