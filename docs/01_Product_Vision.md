# FinanceAI — Product Vision & Design Philosophy

**Document Version:** 1.0.0
**Status:** Official Source of Truth

## Vision
FinanceAI is an enterprise-grade AI-powered Personal Finance Operating System that minimizes manual work through automation.

## Product Principles
- Zero Manual Entry
- AI First
- Privacy First
- Offline First
- Enterprise Quality

## Target Users
- Professionals
- Freelancers
- Small Businesses
- Families

## Long-Term Goal
Unify budgeting, expense tracking, tax planning, investments, trip expenses, financial health and AI assistance into a single platform.
